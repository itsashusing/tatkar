About Us

Tatkaar Kala Pradarshan Sansthan is a premier performing arts institute dedicated to preserving and promoting the Indian classical heritage. We specialize in Kathak dance, Indian classical & Bollywood music, and performance arts. Our mission is to nurture talent, boost confidence, and connect students with India's cultural legacy.

Benefits of Dance & Music Diploma

  • Helps you become a dancer, trainer, artist, or choreographer.
  • Builds self-confidence and improves stage presence.
  • Opens doors to government teaching jobs.
  • Enhances rhythm and coordination.
  • Connects you with Indian culture and traditions.
  • Increases employment chances in the music industry (as a composer, producer, or teacher).
  • Suitable for all age groups, engaging and energetic.
